# Day 9: Network Auditing with Nmap

## Task 1 – Install Nmap
`pkg install nmap -y`

## Task 2 – Scan Your Own Device
`nmap localhost`

## Task 3 – Scan Your Local Network (optional)
`nmap -sn 192.168.1.0/24` (replace with your network)

## Screenshot Required
Show the output of `nmap localhost`

## Warning
Only scan networks you own or have permission to test.

## Absolute Truth
You cannot defend what you cannot see.
